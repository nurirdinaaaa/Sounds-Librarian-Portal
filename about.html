Nur Aisyah Binti Zulkifli

Nur Aisyah is an aspiring librarian and audio enthusiast who brings stories to life through sound. As a sound-focused librarian, she ensures every voice is heard — literally.

  • Role: Audio Engineer
  • Duties: Edits, restores, and ensures high-quality sound recordings for public listening.
  • Equipment Expertise: Directional microphones, mixers, and DAWs like Audacity and Pro Tools.
  • Skills: Studio recording techniques, editing, noise reduction, and mastering.
  • Main Goal: Deliver immersive audio experiences that support inclusive learning and culture preservation.
